WHO WE ARE?

Alpha-Vet (a member of Alpha Holding Investment Co. Ltd.) is a  Hungarian  family-owned  enterprise,  which  was  established  31  years  ago  and  has  been  developing  continuously  ever  since.

Alpha-Vet is market leader in Hungary in the distribution of  veterinary  pharmaceuticals  and  feed  additives,  and  it  has   substantial   manufacturing   capacity   for   veterinary   pharmaceuticals,  feed  additives  as  well  as  crop  and  soil  conditioning  agents.  In  addition  to  the  above,  Alpha-Vet  has  separate divisions for the sale of pet foods and pet accessories, as  well  as  for  livestock  husbandry  equipment  and  veterinary  instruments.

In 2019 the total net sales  turnover  of  Alpha  Holding  was  65  million € (+13.8%), with again the highest operating profits since its establishment, following a trend that has been uninterrupted for many years. It currently provides secure livelihood for over 500 employees.

ALWAYS HIGHER AND HIGHER

PHARMACEUTICALS MANUFACTURING –INNOVATION AND VOLUME

ON SOLID FOUNDATIONS

The Bábolna Pharmaceuticals Manufacturing Plant of Phylaxia Pharmaceuticals Co. Ltd. founded in 1912 has major traditions in the profession. Alpha-Vet started its veterinary pharmaceuticals manufacturing  activity  by  purchasing  the  assets  of  that  renowned  company  in  2008. 

Alpha-Vet completely  renewed  and expanded the Bábolna plant and fitted it up with cutting-edge,  ‘high-tech’  manufacturing  equipment.  Improvement  of  the  manufacturing  conditions  made  it  possible  to  continue  product development and to further improve the quality of the existing product portfolio.

The plant has EU GMP certification, and is now manufacturing veterinary medicinal products for 18 member states of the European Union and further 7 European countries outside the EU.

The veterinary  pharmaceuticals  portfolio  of  Alpha-Vet  is  made up by products for which there is the greatest demand: in  addition  to  antibiotics  and  anthelmintics  for  farm  animals,  the  company  manufactures  own-developed  original  products  unique in the world for treating skin infections and otitis externa in  dogs.  By  reconstruction,  the  plant  was  made  suitable  for  manufacturing  premixes,  powder  mixtures,  pelleted  products,  oral  suspensions / solutions  and  tablets. 

The  manufacturing  capacity of the plant for all types of products is multiple times higher than the Hungarian demand, which ensures the secure supply of products for the export markets and a substantial free capacity for contract manufacturing activity.

Parallel to  the  development  of  our  manufacturing  activity,  our  research  directorate  is  continuously  upgrading  our  own-developed  products  portfolio  in  co-operation  with  numerous  high-ranking European research institutes and universities as partners.
